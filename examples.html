Cyberbullying Examples

Example 1: Social Media Harassment

A user posts a photo on a social media platform, and a group of people start leaving negative comments. They mock the person's appearance and spread rumors, damaging the individual's reputation.

Example 2: Doxxing

Personal information of an individual is posted publicly online without their consent, leading to threats and harassment from strangers.

Example 3: Catfishing

Someone creates a fake online profile to trick another person into believing they are in a real relationship, only to humiliate or exploit them later.
